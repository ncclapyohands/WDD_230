Alignment

Tesla

The moment you open Tesla's home page, it's as if you already know where everything is. It's beautiful background image of the Model 3 allows for a simplistic alignment template. Tesla's logo sitting in the top left, the title of the vehicle centered just above the vehicle, their menu, and even the inventory link hardly moves the eye across the screen while also catching your attention to every corner without even using whitespace.

White Space and Clean Design

Microsoft

The interesting part about Microsoft's website is the website is that it utilizes its Windows operating system styles. They have a white background with a simple list making their page simple and easy to read. They have a semetrical, simple, easy to navigate making this website a perfect example of its flawless execution of white space and clean design.

Repetitive

HP

HP has an amazing example of repetition influencing their page. They have three major products that they want to present to their consumers. These products are what they're most know for. Not only is there organization by using this repetition, but by creating an entire section that divides the three products, it allows the person viewing their page to easy see the product they're looking for.
