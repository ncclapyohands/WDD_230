Our Target Audience

We specifically seek out community members and business owners that want to strengthen our local community. We strive to build local businesses and allow their pressance in our community well known.

The commerce will give businesses opportunities to meet fellow owners that have the same economic, consumer, and community conserns. By meeting and getting to understand one another, we pull all of Portland closer together.

Site Purpose

Our purpose as the Portland Chamber of Commerce is to bring local businesses together. Strength is found in numbers. By bringing the community and our local business, we strengthen our city moto - Keep Portland Weird. This website is for both tourists and locals who have interest in Portland's local businesses and wish to seek them out. We hope to provide opportunities for our community to thrive.
