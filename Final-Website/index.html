How does it work?

    •

Contact Us!

The first step to take is for administration to contact us to see if you're eligible for our program. If you're a business, sign in and begin creating your ad!

The Display

We work with the schools to set up all of the hardware and software necessary to display the slow motion replay. This installation process can happen all within the span of spring break and the best part is all the equipment pays itself off in just two to three years.

Teach

After installing everything, we teach the staff members to use the product. Not only is the software easy to use, but the students can run it themselves. This teaches the students how basic video production works.

The Businesses

Local businesses build their ads right here on the Replay.Live website and, after getting administrative approval, their ad may display in the high school gymnasium.

Why Replay?

Simple for Businesses

You don't need to be tech savvy to use our ad design program. Our advertisement generator allows you to select your favorite template and implement your business into it. You can even upload your own advertisement to our system and we take care of the rest.

Full Package

Replay.Live's goal it to create the one-stop shop for schools and businesses alike. Advertising businesses and changing the entertainment dynamic of the high school gymnasium forever. All right here at the tip of your fingers.

Education

By using Replay.Live, we give the opportunity to students to learn Video Production. After teaching staff, student can enroll to learning how to use slow motion instant replay in their local gyms displaying the talent of the athlete and the student.

School Revenue

Not only are we giving schools a new level of school spirit and home field advantage, but we are also giving them a source of revenue. The program pays itself off and then continues to feed in schools revenue giving Athletic Directors more of a budget for their teams.
